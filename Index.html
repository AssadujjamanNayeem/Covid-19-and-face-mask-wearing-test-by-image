This page is focused on the covid detection and face mask detection and classify that image using backend code. This website will tell us by examine the x-ray image or the face image weather the person has covid or not and the person wearing face mask or not. Test Your X-ray image if you have covid symptom. Test the person wearing the Face Mask or not. and know who we are by clicking About us link About Us

The recognition of human images is not so much about face recognition at all – it is much more image detection finding what includes in the image! It has been proven that the first step in automatic facial recognition – the accurate detection of human images in arbitrary scenes, is the most important process involved. When images can be contain with lots of things, the recognition step afterwards is not so complicated anymore. This is why this page tries to gather all available information about the process of automatically detecting images!
